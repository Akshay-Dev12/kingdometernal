ABOUT US & VISION

The vision of this organization is to lead every soul, without any difference of faith, colour or language, to eternal life, based on the Great Commision of Jesus Christ, found in matthew 28:19, 20 .


STATEMENT OF FAITH

We, the members of this organization believe in Triune God, the Father, in God the Son,Jesus Christ and in God the Holy Spirit. The Heavenly Father, in His love, has sent His Son Jesus Christ to this world to redeem all mankind by His death as a rasmon for their sin, and in His resurrection and ascension into heaven. Also, we believe in the Holy Spirit who comforts all, and in His leadership and fellowship till the second coming of Jesus Christ to recieve His saints.

MINISTRIES

Kingdom eternal global ministries has wider prespectives for a complete growth of mankind, not only spiritual but also physical.This organization marches forward to accomplish it's vison with the following methodolgy.

Spiritual Growth

It is written, "What will it profit a man,if he gains the whole world,and loses his own soul"(Matthew 16:26;Mark 8:36). This emphasizes the spiritual enlightening, by teaching and preaching the word of God, through :-


Gospel Meetings.
Bible study & conferences.
Youth Challenge at schools & colleges.
Empowering the youth.
Healing and Deliverance.

PHYSICAL GROWTH

This is the promise of God that He would bless the bread and water, and take away sickness from among His people (Exodus 23:25; Levi 26:4-13).So-we consider:-


Basic Literacy.
Physical Health-Medical advice/assistance.
Fund raising and co.operative programmes.

STEWARDSHIP

This makes an awareness of the responsibility towards the nature friendly living.Protect the earth which provide food for all mankind.
